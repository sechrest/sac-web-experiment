About [SeattleAngel.com](SeattleAngel.com)

Version .07sa More things. 

SeattleAngel.com is a community guide to the Pacific Northwest angel investing ecosystem.

The site helps founders, investors, and ecosystem builders discover organizations, events, educational resources, and communities across the region.

Seattle serves as the primary hub, showing connections to Portland, Vancouver BC, Kelowna, Spokane, Alaska, and other partner ecosystems.
